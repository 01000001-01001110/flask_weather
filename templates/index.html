About This App

This Hurricane Tracker app fetches weather data every 15 minutes to provide up-to-date information for hurricane tracking.

Use the "View Weather Dashboard" button to see visualizations of the latest weather data.

If you need to refresh the data immediately, use the "Fetch New Data" button.
